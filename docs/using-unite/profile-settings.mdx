# Profile Settings

The **Profile Settings** page allows you to manage your personal account information within Unite.

Use this page to keep your account details up to date, maintain account security, and ensure other workspace members can identify you correctly across the platform.

---

# When to Use Profile Settings

You may need to visit this page when:

- Updating your name or profile information
- Changing your profile picture
- Updating your password
- Reviewing your account role
- Verifying the email address associated with your account

---

# Manage Your Profile

Your profile information is displayed throughout Unite, including:

- User Management
- Notifications
- Activity history
- Support requests

Keeping your profile information current helps team members identify account activity and ownership across pipelines and integrations.

---

# Update Profile Information

You can update personal details such as:

- First name
- Last name
- Profile picture

After making changes, select **Update** to save your profile.

Changes are reflected immediately across the workspace.

---

# Change Your Password

If you need to update your account credentials, use the **Change Password** option.

We recommend changing your password when:

- You suspect unauthorized access
- Your organization's security policies require periodic password updates
- You are using a shared or previously compromised credential

---

# Review Your Account Details

The profile page also displays information associated with your account, including:

- Email address
- Assigned role

Some fields may be managed by workspace administrators and cannot be modified directly.

---

# Best Practices

To keep your account secure:

- Use a strong, unique password
- Update your password periodically
- Keep profile information current
- Avoid sharing login credentials with other users
