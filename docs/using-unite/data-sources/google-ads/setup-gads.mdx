# Connect Google Ads

This guide explains how to connect your Google Ads account as a data source in Unite.

Once connected, you can use your Google Ads data to build pipelines and sync campaign performance data to your destination.

---

## Before you begin

Ensure you have the following:

- A Google account with access to Google Ads.
- A Google Ads account with the required permissions.
- Permission to authorize third-party applications.

---

## Connect your account

### Step 1 — Start the connection

1. Open **Data Sources** from the Unite dashboard.
2. Click **Connect Source**.
3. Select **Google Ads** from the available data sources.

---

### Step 2 — Authorize Google

1. Click **Authorize with Google**.
2. Sign in using your Google account.
3. Review the requested permissions.
4. Click **Continue** and then **Allow** to grant Unite access to your Google Ads account.

---

### Step 3 — Select your account

After authentication:

1. Select the Google Ads account you want to connect.
2. Review the account details.
3. Click **Connect** to complete the setup.

---

## Verify the connection

After the connection is created:

- The data source appears on the **Data Sources** page.
- The connection status changes to **Complete** after validation.
- The connected Google Ads account becomes available when creating pipelines.

---

## Next step

After connecting your Google Ads account, create a pipeline to begin syncing campaign data to your destination.
