# Connect Google Search Console

This guide explains how to connect your Google Search Console account as a data source in Unite.

Once connected, you can use Search Console data to build pipelines and sync SEO performance metrics to your destination.

---

## Before you begin

Ensure you have the following:

- A Google account with access to Google Search Console.
- A verified Google Search Console property.
- **Owner** or **Full User** access to the Search Console property you want to connect.
- Permission to authorize third-party applications.

---

## Connect your account

### Step 1 — Start the connection

1. Open **Data Sources** from the Unite dashboard.
2. Click **Connect Source**.
3. Select **Google Search Console** from the available data sources.

---

### Step 2 — Authorize Google

1. Click **Authorize with Google**.
2. Sign in using your Google account.
3. Review the requested permissions.
4. Click **Continue** and then **Allow** to grant Unite access to your Google Search Console account.

---

### Step 3 — Select your property

After authentication:

1. Choose the Google Search Console property you want to connect.
2. Review the selected property.
3. Click **Connect** to finish the setup.

---

## Verify the connection

After the connection is created:

- The data source appears on the **Data Sources** page.
- The connection status changes to **Complete** after validation.
- The connected property becomes available when creating new pipelines.

---

## Next step

After connecting your Google Search Console account, create a pipeline to begin syncing data to your destination.
