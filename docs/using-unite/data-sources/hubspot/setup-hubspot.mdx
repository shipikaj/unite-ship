# Connect HubSpot

This guide explains how to connect your HubSpot account as a data source in Unite.

Once connected, you can build pipelines to automatically sync your CRM, marketing, sales, and customer data to your preferred destination for reporting and analysis.

---

## Before you begin

Ensure you have the following:

- A HubSpot account.
- Permission to access the HubSpot account you want to connect.
- Permission to authorize third-party applications.

---

## Connect your account

### Step 1 — Start the connection

1. Open **Data Sources** from the Unite dashboard.
2. Click **Connect Source**.
3. Select **HubSpot** from the available data sources.

---

### Step 2 — Add your credentials

1. Click **Add Your Credentials**.
2. You will be redirected to HubSpot's authorization page.
3. Sign in to your HubSpot account if prompted.
4. Review the requested permissions.
5. Click **Connect App** to authorize Unite.

After successful authentication, you will be redirected back to Unite.

---

### Step 3 — Select your account

1. Choose the HubSpot account you want to connect.
2. Review the selected account.
3. Click **Connect** to complete the setup.

---

## Verify the connection

After the connection is created:

- The data source appears on the **Data Sources** page.
- The connection status changes to **Complete** after validation.
- The connected HubSpot account becomes available when creating pipelines.

---

## Next step

After connecting your HubSpot account, create a pipeline to begin syncing CRM and marketing data to your destination.
