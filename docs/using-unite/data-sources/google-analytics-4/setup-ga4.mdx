# Connect Google Analytics 4

This guide explains how to connect your Google Analytics 4 (GA4) property as a data source in Unite.

Once connected, you can build pipelines to automatically sync GA4 data to your preferred destination for reporting and analysis.

---

## Before you begin

Ensure you have the following:

- A Google account with access to Google Analytics.
- At least one Google Analytics 4 property.
- The appropriate permissions to access the GA4 property you want to connect.
- Permission to authorize third-party applications.

---

## Connect your account

### Step 1 - Start the connection

1. Open **Data Sources** from the Unite dashboard.
2. Click **Connect Source**.
3. Select **Google Analytics 4** from the available data sources.

---

### Step 2 - Authorize Google

1. Click **Authorize with Google**.
2. Sign in using your Google account.
3. Review the requested permissions.
4. Click **Continue** and then **Allow** to grant Unite access to your Google Analytics account.

---

### Step 3 - Select a GA4 property

After authentication:

1. Select the Google Analytics 4 property you want to connect.
2. Review the selected property.
3. Click **Connect** to complete the setup.

---

## Verify the connection

After the connection is created:

- The data source appears on the **Data Sources** page.
- The connection status changes to **Complete** after validation.
- The connected GA4 property becomes available when creating pipelines.

---

## Next step

After connecting your Google Analytics 4 property, create a pipeline to begin syncing analytics data to your destination.
