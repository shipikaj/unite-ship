# Connect Meta Ads

This guide explains how to connect your Meta Ads account as a data source in Unite.

Once connected, you can build pipelines to automatically sync advertising data from Meta Ads to your preferred destination for reporting and analysis.

---

## Before you begin

Ensure you have the following:

- A Facebook account.
- A Meta Ads account with at least one active Ad Account.
- One of the following roles on the Ad Account:
  - Advertiser
  - Editor
  - Admin
- If the Ad Account belongs to a Meta Business Manager, your Facebook account must be added as a member of that Business Manager.
- Permission to authorize third-party applications.

---

## Connect your account

### Step 1 — Start the connection

1. Open **Data Sources** from the Unite dashboard.
2. Click **Connect Source**.
3. Select **Meta Ads** from the available data sources.

---

### Step 2 — Authorize Meta

1. Click **Authorize with Facebook**.
2. Sign in to your Facebook account if prompted.
3. Review the permissions requested by Unite.

The authorization request includes permissions to:

- Access your email address
- Access your Meta Ads accounts and related advertising data

4. Click **Save** to approve the requested permissions.

You will be redirected back to Unite automatically after the authorization is complete.

---

### Step 3 — Select your Ad Account

After authentication:

1. Select the Meta Ad Account you want to connect.
2. Review the selected account.
3. Click **Connect** to complete the setup.

---

## Verify the connection

After the connection is created:

- The data source appears on the **Data Sources** page.
- The connection status changes to **Complete** after validation.
- The connected Ad Account becomes available when creating pipelines.

---

## Next step

After connecting your Meta Ads account, create a pipeline to begin syncing advertising data to your destination.
