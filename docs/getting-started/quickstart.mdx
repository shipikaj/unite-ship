# Quickstart

Get your first data pipeline up and running in minutes.

Unite works by connecting a **source** to a **destination** and syncing data through a pipeline.

---

## What you'll do

- Create your account  
- Connect a source  
- Connect a destination  
- Run your first pipeline  

---

## 1. Create your account

- Sign up on Unite  
- Verify your email (if required)  
- Log in to your workspace  

You're ready to start.

---

## 2. Connect a source

- Go to **Data Sources**  
- Click **Add source**  
- Select your tool (e.g. Google Ads, Shopify)  
- Authenticate and save  

Your source is now connected.

---

## 3. Connect a destination

- Go to **Destinations**  
- Click **Add destination**  
- Select your destination (e.g. BigQuery, Redshift)  
- Enter credentials and save  

Your destination is ready.

---

## 4. Create a pipeline

- Go to **Pipelines**  
- Click **Create pipeline**  
- Select your source and destination  
- Choose sync frequency  

Your pipeline is created.

---

## 5. Run your first sync

- Open your pipeline  
- Click **Start sync**  

Unite automatically handles extraction, transformation, and loading.

Initial sync time depends on your data size.

---

## Verify your setup

Check your destination for:

- Tables created  
- Data loaded  
- Expected row counts  

---

## Monitor your pipeline

From the dashboard, you can track:

- Sync status  
- Errors (if any)  
- Pipeline health  

---

## What’s next

Now that your first pipeline is live, you can:

- Add more sources  
- Create additional pipelines  
- Set up alerts for monitoring  

---

## Broken pipeline?
 
- Review error logs in the dashboard   
- Reconnect sources or destinations
