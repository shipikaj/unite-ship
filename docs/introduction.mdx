# What is Unite?

Unite is a no-code ETL platform for moving data — from your tools to your data stack — without the usual complexity.

Connect your sources, choose your destination, and let Unite handle the pipeline. No infrastructure. No maintenance.

---

## Why Unite

Most teams don’t struggle with data — they struggle with moving it reliably.

Unite removes that overhead.

- No-code setup, ready in minutes  
- Reliable syncing without constant fixes  
- One place to manage all pipelines  
- Built for both business users and engineers  

---

## What you can do

- Connect tools like Google Ads, Shopify, Meta Ads, and more  
- Send data to BigQuery, Redshift, Looker Studio and more  
- Automate syncs with flexible scheduling  
- Monitor pipeline health and get alerts  
- Keep your team aligned on the same data  

---

## How it works

Unite keeps things simple:

1. **Connect a source**  
   Choose a tool and authorize access.

2. **Select a destination**  
   Your warehouse, database, or spreadsheet.

3. **Configure the pipeline**  
   Define what data to sync and how often.

4. **Run and monitor**  
   Unite handles extraction, transformation, and loading automatically.
