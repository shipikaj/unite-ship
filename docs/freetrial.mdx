# Free trial

Unite offers a 15-day free trial so you can explore the platform and set up pipelines without any upfront commitment.

---

## What’s included

During your trial, you get:

- Access to Starter and Standard plans  
- Full pipeline creation and syncing  
- Ability to connect sources and destinations  
- No credit card required to start  

---

## Adding a payment method

You can add a payment method anytime during the trial.

- You won’t be charged immediately  
- Billing starts only after the trial ends  
- Your pipelines continue without interruption  

---

## What happens when the trial ends

**If a payment method is added:**

- Your account moves to a paid plan  
- Pipelines continue running  

**If no payment method is added:**

- Pipelines are paused  
- No new data is synced  
- You can resume anytime by upgrading  

---

## Data retention

- Your setup is retained for 30 days after the trial ends  
- You can resume without losing configurations  
- Data is removed after 30 days for security  

---

## When to upgrade

Upgrade when:

- You want uninterrupted syncing  
- Your pipelines are in active use  
- You’re ready to move beyond testing
