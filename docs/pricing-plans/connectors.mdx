# Connectors

Unite provides a range of connectors to help you move data from sources to destinations.  
Connector availability varies by plan based on complexity, cost, and performance requirements.

For a full breakdown of plan limits, see: [Limits & Features](./limits-and-features)

---

## Connector Categories

Connectors are grouped based on their availability across plans.

### Standard Connectors
Available in **Starter** and **Standard** plans.

These are typically:
- Simple integrations  
- Lower API complexity  
- Suitable for basic data workflows  

---

### Advanced Connectors
Available in **Standard**, **Premium**, and **Enterprise** plans.

These typically include:
- High-volume data sources  
- More complex APIs  
- Platforms requiring higher sync performance  

---

### Custom Connectors
Available in **Enterprise** plans only.

Used for:
- Private or internal APIs  
- Custom integrations  
- Specialized data workflows  

---

## Destination Access

Destination availability depends on your plan:

- **Starter** → Limited destination support  
- **Standard and above** → Access to all supported destinations  

---

## Why Connector Access Is Limited

Some connectors are restricted to higher-tier plans due to:

- External API costs and rate limits  
- Increased infrastructure and processing requirements  
- Ongoing maintenance and reliability considerations  

---

## Choosing the Right Plan

If your workflow requires:
- Advanced integrations → Choose **Standard or higher**  
- Custom or internal integrations → **Enterprise** is required
