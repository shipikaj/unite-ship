# Troubleshooting

This guide helps you resolve common issues related to pricing, plan limits, and pipeline usage in Unite.

If you're unsure how billing works, start here: [Billing & Quotas](./billing-and-quotas)

---

## Pipeline Limit Reached

**What’s happening**  
You’ve reached the maximum number of pipelines allowed in your current plan.

**Why this happens**
- Each pipeline counts toward your plan limit when created  
- Deleting a pipeline does not free up quota within the same billing cycle  

**How to resolve**
- Upgrade your plan to increase your pipeline limit  
- Or wait for the next billing cycle when your quota resets  

---

## Unable to Recreate a Pipeline

**What’s happening**  
You are unable to recreate a pipeline that was previously deleted.

**Why this happens**
- Pipelines are tracked historically within the billing cycle  
- Recreating a previously deleted pipeline does not count as a new available slot  

**How to resolve**
- Create a new pipeline instead  
- Or upgrade your plan if you’ve already reached your limit  

---

## Pipelines Are Paused

**What’s happening**  
Your pipelines are no longer syncing.

**Why this happens**
- Your free trial has ended  
- Or your plan quota has been exceeded  

**How to resolve**
- Upgrade your plan to resume syncing  

---

## Connector Not Available

**What’s happening**  
You’re unable to access or use a specific connector.

**Why this happens**
- Some connectors are only available in higher-tier plans  

**How to resolve**
- Upgrade your plan to access advanced or custom connectors  

---

## Still Need Help?

If your issue isn’t covered here:
- Review your usage in the dashboard  
- Check your current billing cycle and plan limits  
- Reach out to us at: **support@uniteyourdata.com**
