# Plans

Unite uses a **plan-based pricing model** combined with **pipeline-based usage limits**.

Unlike traditional ETL tools that charge based on data volume, Unite pricing is primarily based on:
- Pipelines created  
- Data sources and accounts connected  
- Feature access  

To understand how usage is calculated, see: [Billing & Quotas](./billing-and-quotas)

## Plan Guidance

- **Starter** — suitable for initial setup and small datasets  
- **Standard** — recommended for most teams and regular workflows  
- **Premium** — designed for larger workloads and scaling needs  
- **Enterprise** — tailored for advanced requirements and custom setups  

---

## Billing and Renewal

- Plans are billed on a **monthly or yearly cycle**, depending on your selection  
- Billing starts immediately after upgrading to a paid plan  
- Plans **automatically renew** at the end of each billing cycle  
- Upgrades take effect immediately and reset your billing cycle  
- Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle  

---

## Free Trial

- 15-day free trial (no plan selection required)  
- Starts when you create or run your first pipeline  
- Includes access similar to the Standard plan  

After the trial ends:
- Pipelines are paused  
- New actions are restricted until you upgrade
