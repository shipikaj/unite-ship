# Billing, Cancellation & Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** February 6, 2026  

At Unite, we want your data to flow smoothly and your experience with us to be just as seamless. Because our platform handles automated data processing and dedicated resource allocation, we’ve put together this policy to stay transparent, fair, and most importantly human.

---

## 1. Subscription & Cancellation

You’re always in the driver’s seat. You can cancel your Unite subscription at any time.

- **How to Cancel:** No hoops to jump through. Just head to your Account Settings > Billing or send a quick note to our support team at shantanu@statfinity.com.  

- **Simple Cancellation:** If you cancel today, you’ll keep full access to your pipelines until the end of your current billing period, and you simply won’t be charged again.  

- **Data Retention:** After your subscription expires, we’ll keep your configurations safe for 30 days in case you decide to come back. After that, we’ll wipe them to keep our systems (and your data) secure. Checkout our Privacy policy and Terms of Service for more details.

---

## 2. The 15-Day Free Trial & Billing

Fall in love with Unite. Get 15 days free — that’s half a month on us!

- **Risk-Free Testing:** Your first 15 days are completely free. We don't ask upfront for a card to explore our Starter and Standard plans. To unlock the Premium plan during the trial, you’ll simply need to add a card - you won’t be charged during the trial period.  

- **Adding a Card:** If you decide to stick with us and add your card on or before the 15th day, you won't be charged immediately. Your first payment is processed only when your 15-day trial (360 hours) officially ends.  

- **Automatic Enrollment:** Once your trial ends, if a card is on file, you’ll be automatically moved to your chosen paid plan so your data pipelines don't skip a beat.

---

## 3. Trial Expiration

If you haven't added a card by the end of your 15-day trial:

- **Service Pause:** Your data pipelines will automatically pause at the end of the 360th hour. No new data will sync until a plan is activated.  

- **The "Save My Work" Window:** We will safely store your connectors and configurations for 30 days after your trial ends.  

- **Coming Back:** If you add a card within those 30 days, you can resume syncing exactly where you left off. After 30 days, we clear expired trial data to keep our systems secure.

---

## 4. Refunds

In the world of data syncing, costs start the moment a pipeline runs. Because of this, payments are generally non-refundable. However, we aren’t robots—we know things happen.

We will make it right if:

- **Technical Glitches:** If a confirmed error on our side keeps you from using Unite for more than 48 hours, we’ll issue a pro-rated credit or refund.  

- **The "New Fit" Rule:** If you’re a new customer and realize within 15 days of your first payment that Unite isn’t for you, let us know. As long as you haven't moved a massive amount of data (over 500,000 rows), we’ll give you a full refund.  

- **Double Billing:** If our system accidentally charges you twice, we’ll fix it within 48 hours.

---

## 5. Usage & Plan Limits

No auto-upgrades. No surprise charges. Our plans are based on how much data you move.

- **Hitting Your Limit:** If you reach your plan’s data limit, your syncs may pause. We will never automatically upgrade you or charge you extra without your permission.  

- **Upgrading:** To keep your data moving, you’ll need to manually upgrade to a higher tier through your billing dashboard.  

- **Non-Refundable:** Once you choose to upgrade or pay for a higher plan, we instantly allocate extra infrastructure to support your usage. Because of this, upgrade charges aren’t refundable.

---

## 6. Keeping This Policy Fresh

We might update this policy as we grow. If we make big changes, we’ll let you know via email or your dashboard. Continuing to use Unite after an update means you agree to the revised terms.

Have a unique situation? If you have a problem that isn't covered here, please reach out to us at shantanu@statfinity.com. We’d always prefer an honest conversation over leaving you frustrated!
